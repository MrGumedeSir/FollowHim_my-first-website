Our Purpose

We are a mobile-first rehabilitation solution designed to meet you where you are, combining compassion, science, and community to empower your journey to recovery.

Our Vision

To create a dynamic, mobile-first substance rehabilitation solution that meets clients where they are—physically, emotionally, and spiritually—while using mobile technology as a tool for healing.

Our Mission

To empower individuals battling addiction through personalized, mobile-based support that combines compassion, science, community outreach, and holistic wellness—delivered directly to their doors.

Why "Follow Him"

It's a call towards healing and purpose, playing on "following" in digital culture to redirect it toward hope, growth, and client-led, non-judgmental recovery.

Understanding the Challenge

We provide help for a wide range of dependencies by addressing both the substance and the common effects.

Substance Categories Addressed

Artificial Substances

  • Alcohol
  • Dagga
  • Cocaine
  • Tik
  • Cigarettes

Neurochemical Dependencies

  • Dopamine & Serotonin
  • GABA & Melatonin
  • Norepinephrine
  • Choline & Glutamate

Common Effects of Dependence

Speech: Slurred, stuttering, blocked expression

Thoughts: Foggy, confused, paranoid

Feelings: Numb, unstable, extreme highs/lows

Sleep: Insomnia or oversleeping

Behavior: Risky, aggressive, withdrawn

Healing at your pace, in your space

We understand that healing is not a one-size-fits-all journey — it takes time, compassion, and a safe environment where individuals feel seen and supported. That's why we bring rehabilitation to you, creating a space where you can heal emotionally, spiritually, and mentally without being uprooted from your comfort zone. Our mobile-first approach allows each person to engage with recovery on their own terms, with dignity and grace, while receiving consistent guidance, support, and community care. Whether you are at home, in transition, or seeking a fresh start, Follow Him walks beside you— empowering you to take the next step when you're ready.
